Who is Coldplay


Coldplay is a British pop rock and alternative rock band formed in London in 1996.6 7 It is made up of Chris Martin, Jon Buckland, Guy Berryman and Will Champion. It is one of the most relevant groups of the 2000s and early 2010s.
